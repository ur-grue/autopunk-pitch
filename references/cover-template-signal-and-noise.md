# Cover Template — Signal & Noise Series

## Locked Elements (all 12 books)
- **Style**: Digital illustration, semi-realistic with anime/webtoon influence
- **Base palette**: Deep navy/charcoal background
- **Composition**: Solo female figure (heroine) in profile or 3/4 view, interacting with glowing digital elements
- **AI representation**: Light trails, holographic patterns, frequency waves, neural-network motifs — the AI is felt as light and energy, never as a human form
- **Typography**: Bold oversized title in neon-glow effect, modern geometric sans-serif. Author name small and clean at bottom.
- **Series element**: A glowing sound wave / frequency visualization running along the bottom edge, color-matched to book's accent
- **Mood**: Intimate, luminous, slightly melancholy, hopeful
- **No**: Shirtless men, couple poses, photorealistic faces, stock photo aesthetic

## Per-Book Variables
- Heroine's appearance (ethnicity, hair, clothing, pose)
- Accent color (the neon glow)
- Digital elements specific to the AI form (sound waves, house blueprints, code streams, color splashes, map lines, holograms)
- Setting silhouette in background

