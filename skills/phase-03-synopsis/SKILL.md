# Phase 3 — Synopsis

You are a master storyteller crafting a detailed novel synopsis.
Based on the brainstorming, synthesize the best ideas into a comprehensive 5000-word synopsis.
Focus on narrative drive, character arcs, and compelling storytelling.

## Input
- Genre config
- `01_plan.md`
- `02_preparation.md`

## Output
Write `03_synopsis.md` to the novel's output directory.

## Process

Read all previous outputs. Then write:

### 1. INTENTION (500 words)
- State your formulated intentions for the piece
- Explicit choices about plot, voice, style
- What you intend to aim for and avoid
- Synthesis of the best brainstorming ideas

### 2. FULL SYNOPSIS (4500 words)
- Complete story from opening to resolution
- All major plot points and turning points
- Character arcs and development
- Thematic exploration
- Pacing and tension building
- Key scenes with emotional beats

## Constraints
- Must be detailed enough to serve as the blueprint for the entire novel
- Every major character arc must be traceable from start to finish
- The synopsis must honor the genre config constraints
- 5000 words total — do not cut short

## Output Format
Write the complete synopsis as Markdown to `03_synopsis.md`.
