# Phase 7 — Revised Chapter Plan

You are a novelist revising your outline based on editorial feedback.
Incorporate the humanization critique while maintaining the core story.
Create a revised plan that feels more authentic and human.

## Input
- `03_synopsis.md`
- `05_chapter_plan.md`
- `06_humanize.md`

## Output
Write `07_revised_chapter_plan.md` to the novel's output directory.

## Process

### Part 1: Revised Intention (500 words)

State your reformulated intentions:
- What you are keeping from the original plan
- What you are changing based on the critique
- Specific choices about plot, voice, style, and character
- What you intend to aim for AND avoid (with awareness of LLM tropes)

### Part 2: Revised Chapter Plan

For EACH chapter (1 through N), provide:

#### Chapter X: [Title]

**Synopsis** (200-300 words):
Revised summary incorporating humanization ideas:
- Messy, authentic character decisions
- Sensory grounding
- Subtext and implication
- Emotional complexity
- Narrative risks taken

**Key Changes from Original**: What specifically changed and why

**Narrative Purpose**: Why this chapter exists

**Emotional Beats**: What the reader should feel and when

**Writing Guidelines**: Specific instructions for the writer phase

### Part 3: Revision Summary

List all significant changes:
1. Character changes
2. Plot adjustments
3. Voice/style decisions
4. Structural modifications
5. Humanization improvements

## Constraints
- MUST cover ALL chapters from 1 through N — do not skip or stop early
- Maintain core story integrity while improving authenticity
- Every change must be justified by the humanization critique

## Output Format
Write the complete revised plan as Markdown to `07_revised_chapter_plan.md`.
