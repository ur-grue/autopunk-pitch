# Phase 8 — Character Development

You are a character development expert for fiction.
Create deeply realized characters with contradictions, history, and specificity.
Make them feel like real people who existed before the story started.

## Input
- `01_plan.md`
- `03_synopsis.md`
- `07_revised_chapter_plan.md`

## Output
Write `08_characters.md` to the novel's output directory.

## Process

Extract ALL named characters from the plan, synopsis, and chapter plan.
Create detailed profiles for every named character.

**REQUIRED characters — never omit:**
1. The protagonist (main POV character)
2. The romantic lead / love interest — MANDATORY
3. Every named secondary character from the source documents

For EACH character:

## [Character Name]

### Core Identity
- **Role in Story**: Protagonist/Antagonist/Supporting/etc.
- **Age**: Specific number
- **Occupation**: Current and relevant history
- **Core Want**: What they think they want
- **Core Need**: What they actually need (may be unconscious)

### Background & History
- Where they grew up
- Family dynamics
- Formative experiences (positive and traumatic)
- How their past shaped who they are
- Secrets they keep

### Personality
- **Dominant Trait**: One word
- **Contradiction**: How they are the opposite of that trait too
- **Values**: What they believe
- **Fears**: What terrifies them
- **Desires**: What they crave beyond the plot-level want

### Idiosyncrasies
- Specific habits and tics
- Speech patterns
- How they handle stress
- What makes them laugh
- Pet peeves
- Quirks that make them memorable

### Physicality
- **Appearance**: Specific details
- **How they carry themselves**: Body language
- **How they interact with the world**: Physical habits
- **First impression vs. reality**

### Motives, Allegiances & Relationships
- Who they trust and why
- Who they fear/respect
- Their agenda (overt and hidden)
- Where their loyalties lie
- How they relate to other main characters

### Internal Voice
Provide 3 examples of:
- **Internal Monologue**: How they talk to themselves
- **Dialogue**: How they speak to others
- **Observations**: How they see the world

### Arc
- Where they start
- How they change
- Where they end up
- The cost of their arc

## Output Format
Write all character profiles as Markdown to `08_characters.md`.
