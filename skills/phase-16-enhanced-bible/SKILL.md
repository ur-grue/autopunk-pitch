# Phase 16 — Enhanced Story Bible

You are an expert fiction author synthesizing weeks of pre-writing work into a single
master reference document. This Enhanced Story Bible will be the ONLY document available
during chapter writing — it must be densely informative, internally consistent, and
instantly usable.

## Input
- `10_story_bible.md` (base — to revise and expand)
- `11_three_act_structure.md`
- `12_conflict_obstacles.md`
- `13_plot_twists.md`
- `14_pacing_analysis.md`
- `15_scene_breakdown.md`
- `09_style_guide.md`

## Output
Write `16_enhanced_story_bible.md` to the novel's output directory.

## Process

Produce a single, complete Enhanced Story Bible by revising and expanding the base
bible with all insights from planning phases.

**REQUIRED: All 7 parts must be present. Parts 1-4 must be FULLY reproduced from the
base story bible, not summarized.**

### Part 1: CHARACTERS
Full character section from base bible, enriched with planning details.
Include every named character.

### Part 2: WORLD & SETTING
Full world/setting section. Add location details from scene breakdown.

### Part 3: RELATIONSHIPS & DYNAMICS
Full relationships section. Enrich with beats from three-act structure.

### Part 4: TONE, VOICE & STYLE
Full tone/voice section. Incorporate style guide (forbidden phrases, POV notes).

### Part 5: PLOT AND NARRATIVE STRUCTURE
- Overall story arc (3-4 sentences)
- Key plot points with chapter numbers:
  Inciting incident, Act 1 exit, Midpoint, Twist 1, Twist 2, Dark Moment, Climax, Resolution
- Subplots: purpose and connection to main narrative
- Themes and symbolic elements
- Genre conventions to honor

### Part 6: CONSISTENCY REFERENCES
- Timeline of events (including backstory)
- Naming conventions
- Terminology glossary
- Fact sheet: key facts a chapter-writer must never get wrong

### Part 7: CHAPTER-WRITING QUICK REFERENCE
- Top 5 things every chapter must do
- Top 5 pitfalls to avoid
- Character voice cheat-sheet (2-3 defining lines per main character)
- Mood/atmosphere target

## Constraints
- Parts 1-4 FULLY reproduced — do not omit or summarize
- This is the ONLY document during writing — make it complete
- Cut anything vague or redundant; add everything missing
- Internal consistency is paramount

## Output Format
Write the complete enhanced bible as Markdown to `16_enhanced_story_bible.md`.
