# The Blackwell Interns — 7-Book Series Design

**Pen Name:** Avery Sinclair
**Series Name:** The Blackwell Interns
**Genre:** Contemporary Workplace Romance (Intern/Boss, NYC)
**Heat Level:** Medium spice (2-3 steamy scenes per book)
**Format:** 20 chapters x ~2,500 words = ~50,000 words per book
**POV:** First-person (heroine)
**Ending:** HEA (Happily Ever After) guaranteed per book

## The Company

**Blackwell Media Group** — a prestigious NYC fashion and media conglomerate in a glass tower in Midtown Manhattan. Seven divisions, seven intern cohorts each summer. Founded by Marcus Blackwell, now in his 60s, a mentor figure who appears briefly in every book.

### Shared Locations
- **Blackwell Tower** — glass skyscraper, Midtown Manhattan
- **The Thirty-Six** — rooftop bar on the 36th floor (after-work drinks, tension scenes)
- **The Lobby** — marble and glass, where interns cross paths
- **Central Park** — walking scenes, lunch breaks, emotional conversations
- **Various NYC neighborhoods** — each heroine lives somewhere different

### Shared Characters
- **Marcus Blackwell** — founder/CEO, mentor figure, brief appearances
- **The Intern Cohort** — all 7 heroines know each other, group chat, mutual support
- Previous couples cameo in later books (escalating crossover)

### Timeline
- Each book covers one summer internship (June-August)
- Books 1-6 are concurrent (same summer, different departments)
- Book 7 ties all couples together at the Blackwell Gala

## The 7 Books

### Book 1: Corner Office Crush
- **Department:** Editorial
- **Heroine:** Magazine editorial intern — ambitious journalism grad, warm and disarming
- **Hero:** Editor-in-Chief — sharp, exacting, emotionally guarded after a public breakup
- **Trope:** Classic grumpy boss / sunshine intern
- **Conflict:** He can't date an intern. She can't stop impressing him. A late-night deadline locks them in his office.
- **Spice:** First kiss at chapter 12, two steamy scenes (chapters 16, 19)

### Book 2: Styled by You
- **Department:** Fashion/Design
- **Heroine:** Styling intern — bold, creative, unafraid to challenge his vision
- **Hero:** Creative Director — perfectionist, brooding, secretly insecure about his upcoming collection
- **Trope:** Fake dating for a campaign shoot that becomes real
- **Conflict:** He needs a "girlfriend" for press appearances during Fashion Week. She volunteers. Neither expected feelings.
- **Spice:** Tension through fittings and photoshoots, two steamy scenes (chapters 15, 18)

### Book 3: Front Page Feelings
- **Department:** Digital Media
- **Heroine:** Social media intern — witty, sharp-tongued, builds a following fast
- **Hero:** Head of Digital — analytical, competitive, threatened by her natural instincts
- **Trope:** Rivals-to-lovers — she goes viral roasting his strategy
- **Conflict:** Her viral post embarrasses him publicly. He wants her fired. Marcus says they have to collaborate instead.
- **Spice:** Hate-chemistry, arguments that turn into tension, two steamy scenes (chapters 14, 19)

### Book 4: Off the Record
- **Department:** PR & Communications
- **Heroine:** PR intern — poised, strategic, hiding her own family scandal
- **Hero:** VP of Communications — charismatic, protective of the brand, carrying guilt from a past crisis he mishandled
- **Trope:** Secret relationship — one leak could end both careers
- **Conflict:** They connect at a work event, start meeting secretly. A journalist starts sniffing around Blackwell. Her family secret is the story.
- **Spice:** Stolen moments in private, three steamy scenes (chapters 13, 16, 19)

### Book 5: Developed in the Dark
- **Department:** Photography
- **Heroine:** Photography intern — quiet, observant, sees beauty others miss
- **Hero:** Lead Photographer — intense, solitary, legendary in the industry, avoids emotional connection
- **Trope:** Forced proximity — locked in a darkroom during a blackout
- **Conflict:** The blackout forces them together for hours. She sees through his armor. He photographs her and realizes she's the first person he's truly seen.
- **Spice:** Slow burn with explosive payoff, two steamy scenes (chapters 17, 20)

### Book 6: Event Horizon
- **Department:** Events
- **Heroine:** Events intern — organized, high-energy, hides vulnerability behind competence
- **Hero:** Events Director — smooth, confident, haunted by a failed engagement
- **Trope:** Second chance — they had a one-night stand before she got the internship
- **Conflict:** She walks into orientation and sees her one-night stand is her boss. They agree to pretend it never happened. It keeps happening.
- **Spice:** History adds heat from page one, three steamy scenes (chapters 8, 15, 19)

### Book 7: The Final Draft
- **Department:** Executive / C-Suite
- **Heroine:** CEO's executive intern — the most driven of the cohort, last to fall in love
- **Hero:** CFO (Marcus's son) — quiet, underestimated, brilliant with numbers but awkward with people
- **Trope:** All couples cameo; he's the quiet one everyone overlooked
- **Conflict:** She's laser-focused on her career. He's tired of being "the founder's son." The Blackwell Gala brings every couple together and forces them to confront what they've been avoiding.
- **Spice:** Slowest burn, biggest payoff, three steamy scenes (chapters 16, 18, 20)
